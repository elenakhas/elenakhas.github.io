Hi! I am Elena. I am a master's student in Computational Linguistics and a member of CLMBR research group.

My journey in NLP and Computational linguistics started from learning to code by myself and then studying Natural Language Processing at University of Lorraine (Nancy, France) and was an intern at SYNALP team at LORIA.

Before going back to graduate school and moving countries, I have been teaching foreign languages for almost a decade as a Fulbright Language Teaching Assistant at University of New Haven (CT, USA), lecturer at Business Foreign Languages department at Saint Petersburg State University (Russia), and various language schools. I did research in conceptual semantics, cognitive linguistics, Siberian dialectology and discourse analysis. These days, I am excited about the potential of technology to generate insights about human languages, and the ways these insights can help Education.
